I remember I was on a dock in the city of Paita (Piura), working in this place with another guy from the university. The two of us had bought the dock, which had been sold at a reasonable price because it was going broke. It happened that other friends from the university came to visit us. We were talking when one of the secretaries came in. One of the guys who was visiting liked her and he asked for the others to come so that we'd all have a partner. Afterwards, an orgy started in which the secretaries got passed around from one to the other. Note: The people were all familiar, good friends of mine. The place was also familiar.
