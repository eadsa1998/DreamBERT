["Unhappy busy doctor."] There is a Doctor who is very busy working in his life. He does good things for other people, teaching them good skills. He feels very sad he is alone and not in relationship. The right one hasn't come along. Meanwhile, in the same room is a woman, the dream ego who is blind. She is writing. They are unaware of each other as relationship people. But they would do well together. They are so busy doing their thing that they don't realize they could love each other and make each other happy.
