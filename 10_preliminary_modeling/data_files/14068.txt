I was at 'school' opening the time capsule. Tiana and Ramona were there, and they were sometimes Calvin and Samantha. Dmitri was still at school. There was a new time capsule in a box. It had Eugene written on it and I was worried someone was going to see it before we opened it. Mr Gutenberg said I had to do a test tomorrow. Jeremiah was there. Some lady's kids were there.
