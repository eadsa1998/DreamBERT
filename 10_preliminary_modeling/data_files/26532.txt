 There is a meeting between Luke Skywalker and Darth Vader. Luke is in water that is enclosed by glass. He seems to be able to breath or to function by holding his breath. The two are civil towards each other at first, talking very seriously. They fight five battles, each with a different weapon. Darth Vader is overpowering in the first two battles. Luke Skywalker uses his skills to win the next three battles and defeats Darth Vader, winning the best of five battles.
