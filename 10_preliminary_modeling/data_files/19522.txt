[Awoke about half hour before time to arise, and during this period dozed off a couple of times, and had these dreams] After leaving one building I entered another which seemed to be some sort of drugstore. The lady behind the counter was dishing out ice cream for a crowd of 8 or 10 children. House flies swarmed, and it seemed that they were to be exterminated by blowing them, by means of an electric fan, into sticky fly paper.
