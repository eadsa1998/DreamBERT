I saw a redecorating TV program quite a few months ago where the man was upset when he came home and saw what they did. This dream was like a follow up to that. It seemed that I changed back and forth in the dream between being myself and being his wife - who had invited the decorator in to do the work while her husband was away. When I was myself in the dream I was trying to get the man to not be angry with his wife. The dream became very sexual in nature.
