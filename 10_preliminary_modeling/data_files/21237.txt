I was watching an old man swinging by one arm. Then he walked on a fence to show how. Agile he was at his age. As he walked, he made a slip and fell off the fence. I said to his daughter, "See how easy he could have broken his neck or arm by showing off. Carter was with us. There were a couple of Japanese whom were going to show us a stunt that was really a good one. Ann was bedridden. She got up and limped on one leg. I felt so sorry for her because I loved her and didn't want to lose her.
