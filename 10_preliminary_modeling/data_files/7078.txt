After they'd left the family gathering, I told H. I'd change my dress now if she wanted me for dinner. She said I could come if I wished but we might run out of conversation. I assured we wouldn't. Decided to put on a pink dress but began to pick up things and dress the two dolls. When I finished the first, I decided I didn't need to do the other as it was nicely dressed in white. Rolled up the remaining dresses and asked H. where to put them and if there was any room in that doll's trunk. "No, but you might put away those socks,: a large pile of unmatched gray socks.
