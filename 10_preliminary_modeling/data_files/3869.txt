["Japanese Concubine."] I am a beautiful Japanese young woman. My mother is encouraging me to flirt with the men officials. They are military men and my mother is acting like a geisha Madame so I can warm them up and get military secrets from them. She is making a prostitute out of me, or very nearly. An American man, like Clark Gable comes and is attracted to me. and I to him. He is very distressed with the way my mother works me. We are leaving to have our own life. We love each other.
