I am going to INISHERE: I am met by an old woman and have to ride in a motor boat that fills up with water; I talk with the old woman who knows some of Wendy's friends; People come to a store where everyone is eager to hear my story! Earlier a young woman wearing a brown hooded dress is eagerly listening to Frank's story; I get angry and throw things at him. He stalks out to the car and drives off with his books in the car.....
