We are up in the MOUNTAINS with many people; I see Joshua (Wendy's husband) and Frank as they walk up in a heavy FOG, but Frank returns where there is a traffic light. (!) - shift: a lovely woman, about my age comes to visit; I like her a lot; when it is time to say good-bye, we hug and fall down and roll downhill on a sandy slope; we laugh and I walk her to her car.
