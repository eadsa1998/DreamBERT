["Cary Grant."] Some rich couples are at a resort. Cary Grant and a woman, young and pretty, are attracted to each other. There is an older woman in her 50's there, and an older man about the same age. They are old friends. However, Cary Grant becomes afraid of the potential marriage with the young woman, even though he loves her. So he decides to become the roommate of the older woman. Now they are planning their wedding. The old man comes to the wedding. He still loves the old woman, so he and the young woman cook up a plot to make their respective lovers jealous. As the wedding is beginning, the old woman looks out the window and sees the old man and the young woman necking on a bench in the garden. This upsets the old woman. She marches out there. Now the couples declare themselves and are properly together with the right ones.
