       -- Set the table and send a scrubby, older woman to get two bottles of wine: while she's gone, Frank and I drive home, forgetting all about the dinner. He says he's not hungry; but I say we have to go back, because we're on the committee; -- Just then a neighbor arrives all dressed up in a black dress and says: "You sure did not help this morning; we did all the cleaning." "But," I say, "I put on the whole dinner."
