I was in my father's club as a guest, and a middle aged woman who had been seduced by me earlier was there. She was very solicitory of me and told me that she still lived in the same place. I told her I would stop up to see her, and next thing I was knocking on her front door. She was very flustered and flighty when she saw me and asked me to come in, and I wondered if any neighbors had seen me come in. We began to embrace, and I woke up.
