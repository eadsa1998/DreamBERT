I was a Carebear from the show "Carebears" and my Carebear friends and I were riding around on rainbows and having a good time. Suddenly the leader of the carebears got really mad at me for no reason and told me I was banished. I hadn't done anything, so I didn't leave. When he found out, he was going to put me in jail, but I got away before he could. Setting: In Carebear Land, up in the sky. Characters: The head Carebear who was evil and mean, and my friends who played with me each day. Feelings and Thoughts: Angry.
